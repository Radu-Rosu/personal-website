My biography

I am Radu Codreanu, currently a 2nd year bachelor student at the Faculty of Automatic Control and Computers from Polytechnic University of Bucharest. I am from Galati, a city in the south-east of Romania. I graduated from the "Vasile Alecsandri" National College in Galati(grade 9.9/10), specialization of mathematics and computer science.

What brought me to this faculty is my passion for tech. Software, hardware, doing scientific research, everything is on my to-do list and I am currently searching for what path should I take for my professional future.

Hobbies

I love gardening and everything related to plants, I love troubleshooting anything that is broken, from toys to cars, and last but not least, I enjoy taking photos of nature, family and friends whenever we feel good.

What makes me feel energetic everytime is thinking about how can I help the world, and with this in my mind I take most of my decisions.
